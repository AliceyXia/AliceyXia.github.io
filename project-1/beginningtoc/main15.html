That sentence begins with your first words,

toddling around the kitchen,

and ends with your last words right before you step into the limousine,

or in a nursing home, the night-duty attendant vaguely on hand.

Or, if you are blessed,

they are heard by someone who knows you and loves you and will be sorry to hear the sentence end.



这句话从你在厨房里蹒跚学步时的第一句话开始，

到你踏上豪华轿车前的最后一句话结束，

或者在养老院里，夜间值班人员依稀在旁。

或者，如果你有福气，

当它们被认识你、爱你的人听到这句话的终止时会感到遗憾。
