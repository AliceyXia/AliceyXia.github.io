Roland Barthes suggests there are three ways to finish any piece of writing:

the ending will have the last word

or the ending will be silent

or the ending will execute a pirouette,

do something unexpectedly incongruent.



罗兰-巴特认为，任何一篇文章都有三种结束方式：

结局会有最后一句话，

或者在结局会沉默不语，

或者在结局会有一个回转，

做一些不协调的出乎意料的举动
